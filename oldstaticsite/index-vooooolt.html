Why KTCA Exists

Comfort becomes complicated when complex medical needs arise.

Everyday comfort isn't simple. We understand the challenges of dressing around PEG tubes, tracheostomies, AFOs (Ankle Foot Orthoses), and foot drop braces. Standard clothing rarely fits the reality of cold limbs during therapy or sensitive skin that cannot tolerate harsh seams.

KTCA was created to change that. Our designs prioritize gentle materials, accessible openings, and non-contact adaptability for monitoring equipment and orthotics.

Medical Friendly

Orthotic Ready

Sensory Soft
